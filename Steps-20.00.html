Adding Visual Charts - Mortality % by Wait Time and Dept 20
This step explains how to create Mortality % by Wait Time and Dept Chart, as shown in the screenshot below

1. To create Mortality % by Wait Time and Dept Chart," click on "Line and Clustered Column Chart,".
2. Next set the x-axis to "Wait Time Category," and column y-axis to "Mortality Rate %,". Then set the line y-axis to "Average Wait Time," and column legend to "Abbreviated Department Referral." 
3. Then, click on the title, add a title name, change the font size to 14, set the color to black, background color to light blue, and align the text to the center. 
4. Finally, click on the legend, turn off the legend title, change the x and y-axis title colors to black, and update the colors of each column representing each department as follows:
5. CAR ? #1B5DAB, GAS ? #6F81AF, GP ? #8F7990, NEU ? #6F4930, ORT ? #4A8DDC, PHY ? #4C5D8A, REN ? #C58358.
